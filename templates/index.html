P2P Loans

P2P Loans is a platform that allows you to either apply for a loan and also able to fund/finance a loan request from your peers. Users of our Peer to Peer Loan Platform are divided into two groups i.e we have

  • Loan Financier

    Every user is required to at least finance a single loan in order to qualify to receive a loan , this is done by first funding your Wallet and then under the Peer to Peer Loans Tab you need to allocate a certain amount of that wallet into your Loan Pocket.

    This will allow other loans to be financed from your loan pocket either automatically or by your choosing.

    The amount in cash of Loans financed from your loan pocket plus fifteen percent becomes your limit when you request for loans in the future.

  • Loan Client

    Now this is the guy who will receive the loan, or is being financed by his loan financier. every loan financier will then be allowed to become a loan client and a limit as to the money he can receive from other loan financiers will be capped at the amount he financed other loans plus fifteen percent on that amount.

    Note: Your loan client will not necessarily become your financier on your next financing round.

    Meaning if you finance your loan to me i do not necessarily have to pay the loan back to you, i just have to pay it back to someone within the platform
