How to WithDraw Funds

if you had already set the auto withdraw option on the settings tab of your profile or wallet then there is no need to use this form to request a withdrawal as all your available funds will be withdrawn to your paypal account on the next payment cycle.
Using this form you can manually set the exact amount you want withdrawn at the next payment cycle, this means that this form will overwrite auto withdrawal settings and then only withdraw the amount requested in this form
